# Privacy Policy for QuickMoov

**Last updated: January 28, 2026**

## Overview

QuickMoov is a macOS application that optimizes video files for streaming. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**QuickMoov does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- No user accounts required
- No analytics or tracking
- No network connections
- No data sent to external servers

## File Processing

- All file processing is performed locally on your device
- Your video files never leave your computer
- Original files remain untouched; only copies are modified

## Third-Party Services

QuickMoov does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub: [@kyungw00k](https://github.com/kyungw00k)
